Dr.Abdul Kalam

The missile man of India

Dr. A.P.J abdul kalam, also the Former President of India.


Here's a time line of Dr. Kalam's life:


  • 1931 - Born in Tamil muslim family of rameshwaram,India
  • 1960 - Graduated from Madras Institute of technology
  • 1961 - joined the Aeronautical Development Establishment of the Defence Research and Development Organisation (by Press Information Bureau, Government of India) as a scientist after becoming a member of the Defence Research & Development Service (DRDS).
  • 1969 - Kalam was transferred to the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) where he was the project director of India's first Satellite Launch Vehicle
  • 1970-1990 - Kalam made an effort to develop the polar satellite
  • 2002 - Kalam served as the prsident of India.He is also the first scientist to become the President of India Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam was one of the most revered figures from the Rashtrapati Bhavan. He was known as the “people’s President”, and was adored by almost everyone.
  • 2015 - kalam died in shillong while delivering lecture in Indian institute of Management due to cardiac arrest
